About Me

Naturally curious, boundless creativity, unending determination.

Meynard A.D. Sabate is an aspiring Full-Stack Web Developer. Gravitating toward technology at a young age, he has always had a curiosity that can never be quenched. From taking household appliances apart & putting them back together, to building computers & setting up personal networks in his home. A natural problem-solver who enjoys creating & developing aesthetically appealing websites, apps, & software. Meynard is a firm believer in ease of access & easy-to-navigate, streamlined user-interfaces. He hopes to help you build your future websites & apps with this design philosophy in mind.
